Please read the following instructions for proper test run before you begin it:

  1. If you are not very experienced in graph here are some notions that might be helpful:
    • What is it a graph?
    • What is it a node?
    • What is it an edge?
  2. Please fill in this questionnaire using a desktop or laptop computer, not a tablet or phone. Use the pointing device you are most comfortable with (for example, a mouse or touchpad).
  3. Press F11(Windows) or Ctrl & ⌘ & F(Mac) to enter in full-screen mode. If you want to stop the questionnaire at any point, use the same hotkey to exit full-screen mode.
  4. It is important that you can easily read the text and clearly see the figures. Fit your browser size so you can see the entire image on the left:
    • for Windows and Linux: press Ctrl & + to grow or Ctrl & - to shrink.
    • for Mac: press ⌘ & + or ⌘ & -.
  5. Between a question and another there will be an intermediate page where time will not be measured. If you are forced to stop for some problem taking advantage of these moments!
  6. Please do not press the back button of the browser during the test run.
  7. Please make sure that JavaScript is activated in your browser.
  8. Do not complete the questionnaire by giving random answers. If you want to stop, simply close your tab or browser.
  9. You will encounter three types of questions:
    • Closed-ended questions where you will choose the answer between various options.
    • Open answer questions.
    • Questions in which the answer will be given by clicking through the requested nodes or edges.
  10. In all questions, you can zoom the image with the mouse wheel and move it by clicking and dragging it.
