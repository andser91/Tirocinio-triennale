In the test you will encounter three graph design styles:

L-Drawing

- The highlighted line is an edge that connect the node 2 to the node 1

- The highlighted node(node 2) is the neighbour in common between the node 3 and the node 10

Overloaded-Orthogonal

- The highlighted line is an edge that connect the node 1 to the node 7

- The highlighted node(node 2) is the neighbour in common between the node 7 and the node 10

Matrix

- The blue rects are edge

- The highlighted rect is an edge that connect the node 10 to the node 4

- The highlighted node(node 2) is the neighbour in common between the node 1 and the node 7
